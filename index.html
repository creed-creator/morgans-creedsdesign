Our Story

A Neighborhood
Bar Done Right

Morgan's Tap & Grill is Fort Wayne's locally owned neighborhood bar and grill on the south side. The kind of place where the owners know your name, the burgers are hand-smashed fresh, and the beer is always cold. Come for the food, stay for the atmosphere.

Smash Burgers

Hand-smashed fresh beef patties — crispy edges, juicy center.

Wings

Crispy wings your way. Sauced or dry, they're the real deal.

Baskets & Apps

Shrimp, chicken, fish baskets plus fried pickles and onion rings.

Full Bar

Cold drafts, cocktails, and a full spirits menu to wash it all down.
